Epic Pep Rally Sparks Excitement for Upcoming Sports Season

Hey Cheesemeisters! The gymnasium was on fire yesterday as our school hosted the most epic pep rally ever! The entire student body gathered to show support for our amazing Kickball Team. Cheerleaders flipped through the air, the band played "Feels So Good" by Chuck Mangione, and Principal Fullerton performed a monologe from King Lear! We're all buzzing with excitement for the upcoming Kickball season. Let's go, Cheesemeisters!

Art Extravaganza Takes Over School Hallways

Last week, our hallways transformed into an art lover's paradise. The Art Club organized an incredible art extravaganza, featuring masterpieces from our talented student artists. Paintings, sculptures, and multimedia installations turned our school into a vibrant gallery. Boorelynn Knudsen showcased her newest edible sculpture, "Dimensions of Promise," made entirely out of locally made Gouda. Needless to say it didn't last long after the exibit finished! "Goudos" to the artists for sharing their creativity with us!

Our Student Council is crushing it! They organized a charity drive that exceeded all expectations. The spirit of giving is alive and well at our school, thanks to the amazing efforts of Student Council!
